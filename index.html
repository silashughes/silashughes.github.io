My name is Silas Hughes, and I am an Engineer, a Roboticist, and an UAV Pilot.

I consult with companies in the Greater Boston area, helping teams design, prototype, build, and test their robotic systems. I specialize in additive manufacturing (3D printing) and unmanned air vehicles (drones).


I received my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Olin College of Engineering. I continue to assist with robotics research conducted at Olin College.
